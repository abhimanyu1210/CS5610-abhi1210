Features Of Healthy Gobbler

1) Search Food Items

  • Unregistered Users:
    • Users who are not registered as members of the application can try out the features of this application. The user can look up the calorie content of any meal they consume. The search engine is powered by Yummly and returns a bulk of options for users to choose from.
    • If they find a match on the meal, they can look up the meal details. Meal details include information like the calorie content, the image of the meal and an accurate list of instructions on how the meal was prepared. To enjoy other features of the app the users have to register.
  • Registered Users:
    • Registered Users don't just get static information about the meals they have on daily basis. They can learn their recommended calorie intake per day by providing essential details like height and weight and their lifestyle. All of the features listed below are available to registered users.
 

2) Logging Meals

  • Once they know their recommended intake, they can look up their meals and log them. This will change the calories remaining for that day. Users can plan their rest of the day's diet accordingly.
  • Users can search the food items they consumed and log it in the application. The application shows a progress bar to the user showing his calories remaining for the day.
 

3) Logging Exercises

  • Registered users also have an option of logging the calories that they burned. There is an exercise tracker option where users can choose from a list of exercise. The application will calculate the calories burned for them. Calories burned and gained work as two sides of the same coin.
  • The list of exercises provided covers most of the routine workouts we do. The formula to calculate the calories is scientifically proven accurate and can be found on "American Health Information Management" website.
 

4) Social Connections and Activity Feed

  • The best part of the application is connecting with other users. People who join this application will post reviews on food-items on how they have good or bad health effects.
  • Registered users can follow other users and see their activity on their home page.
  • Through socials connections, keeping yourself healthy becomes a collective effort.
 

5) Favorites

  • Users can also keep a track of their favorite meals.
  • Meals stored as favorites act as a quick access to calorie information and instructions on how to cook that meal.
  • Users have access to all of their past data. They can go back and check their calorie intake for any past date for which they logged their meals and exercises.
