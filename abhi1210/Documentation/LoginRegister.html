Login/Register Page

The login and register page is an entry point for account holders of this web application. Users who have not registered yet have an option of navigating to the register page to fill out all the necessary details.

If the user is a registered user, he can put his credentials in and login to the application. On login, the user will be taken to his personal dashboard page.

In the register page, the person is asked to put in his age, height and weight for calorie calculation purposes. Routing between the pages is smooth and animated giving an overall good UI experience.
