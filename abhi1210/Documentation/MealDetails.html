Meal Details Page

The meal details page is accessible to both registered and unregistered users. The only place when an unregistered user is asked for login is when he wants to like that meal, log that meal, comment on it or even see what others have commented.

This page is divided into 3 sections.
  • Meal Information
    • Name of the meal
    • Number of comments on the meal
    • Number of likes on the meal
    • Snapshot of the meal
    • Instruction to cook the meal
  • Meal Logging
    • Calorie content of the meal
    • Choosing date on which the meal was consumed
    • Logging the meal (Only Registered Users)
  • Comments And Reviews
    • See other comments by users
    • Posting comments and reviews of their own.
