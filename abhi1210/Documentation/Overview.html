Project Overview - "Healthy Gobbler"

1) About "Healthy Gobbler":

 

Target Audience: No age boundaries. Any person who plans to track and control their diet can use this application.

 

"Healthy Gobbler" is an online dietary tracker which helps users to track their daily calorie intake. Registered users can see their daily recommended calorie intake, log meals they have and log exercises they do during a day. This application can serve as a hub for people who want to control their diet and stay fit. Users can also connect with people sailing in the same boat. They can follow other users in the application and check their activities which come as a feed to them.

 

2) Why health and fitness?

  • Unhealthy eating habits have contributed to the obesity epidemic in the United States. About one-third of U.S. adults (33.8%) are obese and approximately 17% (or 12.5 million) of children and adolescents aged 2—19 years are obese.
  • A poor diet is associated with major health risks that can cause illness
  • These include:
    • Heart disease
    • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
    • Type 2 diabetes
    • Osteoporosis
    • Certain types of cancer
  • By making smart food choices, you can help protect yourself from these health problems.
